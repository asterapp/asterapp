Aster

Anonymous Text-Based Peer Counseling

Learn More

Features

Chat

Speak anonymously with the various peer counseling groups on campus, such as Room 13 and ECHO.

Learn

Aster provides you with information on the various mental health services available to you on campus.

Safety

In case of an emergency, Aster provides quick access to contact information for a variety of emergency services.

Instant

Our chat engine uses web socket technology. This means that your messages will be sent and received within a few milliseconds.

Easy to Use

Just hit the chat button, select an organization, and you're good to go!

Private

Our service is anonymous, secure, and private. Only you and your counselor will have access to your chat.

Anonymous

Anonymity is maintained at all times. Your name will not be revealed to any counselor.

Secure

End-to-end encryption means that only you and your counselor see each others' messages. In fact, not even our servers can read your conversations!

Private

We take privacy very seriously. None of the chats on Aster are stored in any form.
